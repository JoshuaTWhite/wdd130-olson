Welcome

The Keyblade is one of the most recognizable symbols of the Kingdom Hearts series. While it resembles a key-shaped sword, it is far more than an ordinary weapon. The Keyblade is a mystical instrument capable of choosing its own wielder, protecting hearts, sealing worlds, wielding powerful magic, and much more. Throughout the series, Keyblades play a central role in the battle between light and darkness, making them essential to both the story and gameplay. This website explores what the Keyblade is, how it is obtained, and the many extraordinary abilities it possesses.
